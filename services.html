Truck Dispatching

NewageDispatch is a leading company in truck dispatching services, catering to the needs of owner-operators and smaller trucking firms. Our dedication is to ensure regular supply and help you find the most significant freight load at the cheapest price. We collaborate closely with you to achieve this goal. Our preference lies in partnering with long-term customers instead of transitional ones. We offer comprehensive dispatch services that can help you maximize your earnings, and we follow a straightforward approach to cooperating with our customers according to their specifications. For all your hard work behind the wheel, you deserve the best possible rate per mile, and that’s what our truck dispatchers do. To free you up to focus on growing your business, we’ll take care of all the paperwork associated with your trucks and communicate with brokers on your behalf.

Streamlined Route

Our expert truck dispatchers directly connect you with brokers, freeing up your time to focus on driving! No longer will you need to contact the broker separately for lump sum and detention payments. You have the autonomy to choose your preferred driving destinations, and we are dedicated to securing competitive compensation and ensuring safe driving conditions for you. Access top-tier shippers and brokers who generously reward your services. Our specialized truck dispatcher meticulously scans multiple load boards to find the best dry freight, flatbed, and reefer loads. Say goodbye to vehicle downtime as NewageDispatch keeps you continuously on the move.

Invoicing Service

Invoicing serves as a remarkable strategy to enhance cash flow without accumulating debt. Alongside boosting sales, offering flexible payment arrangements can entice previously unreachable clients. Our trucking dispatch services bring forth numerous advantages for our customers. We understand the pivotal role transportation invoices play in streamlining cash flow and fostering business growth. The commonness of payment under COD conditions in transportation businesses can lead to delays of at least one month. This poses challenges, particularly for smaller carriers who require sufficient financial resources to compensate drivers and replenish fuel supplies. However, with proper invoicing, the burden of unpredictable cash flow can be alleviated.
